\# Orvanthis MVP



Goal:

Build a simple AI opportunity discovery platform.



Core Features:



1\. Opportunity Search

Users can ask the system to find opportunities.



Example:

"Find emerging AI infrastructure companies"



2\. AI Opportunity Report

The AI generates a structured opportunity report.



Report sections:

\- Summary

\- Opportunity Score

\- Why This Matters

\- Bull Case

\- Bear Case

\- Risks



3\. Opportunity Score

Each opportunity gets a score from 1–10.



4\. Saved Reports

Users can save reports for later.



Do NOT build yet:

\- alerts

\- portfolios

\- advanced analytics

\- mobile apps
