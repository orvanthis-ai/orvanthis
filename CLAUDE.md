\## Project Overview

This repository powers Orvanthis, an AI Strategic Intelligence Platform.



Orvanthis is designed for:

\- founders

\- operators

\- investors

\- consultants

\- executives

\- strategic decision-makers



The goal of the product is to help users:

\- discover strategic opportunities early

\- monitor signals and sectors

\- generate concise intelligence reports

\- track personalized priorities

\- support executive decision-making



This is not a generic chatbot.

This is a premium business intelligence platform.

\## Engineering Rules

When editing this codebase:



1\. Do not break existing working functionality.

2\. Keep code modular and readable.

3\. Prefer simple, practical implementations.

4\. Avoid unnecessary dependencies.

5\. Preserve the premium executive dashboard style.

6\. Keep the product oriented toward business decision-makers.

7\. Avoid introducing toy/demo styling or gimmicks.

8\. Preserve existing localStorage behavior unless intentionally changing it.

9\. Use clear naming and restrained UI patterns.

10\. Do not remove features unless explicitly instructed.



---



\## UI Rules

The interface should feel like:

\- a strategic intelligence terminal

\- a premium executive dashboard

\- a private operator workspace



Design constraints:
